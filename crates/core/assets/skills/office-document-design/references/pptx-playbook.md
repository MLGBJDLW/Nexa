## PPTX Playbook

- Keep one idea per slide and no more than six bullets on any slide.
- Use a narrative arc: title, agenda, problem, evidence, options, recommendation, summary, Q&A.
- Prefer comparison layouts for tradeoff decisions and timeline layouts for plans.
- Put the key message in the slide title, not just the body.
- Add short speaker notes that explain why the slide matters and what to say next.
