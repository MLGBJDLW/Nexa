## DOCX Playbook

- Start with a cover page: title, subtitle, author/date line, and a one-sentence executive summary.
- Use a repeating rhythm for long reports: heading, short framing paragraph, evidence block, then next action.
- Turn any list with three or more comparable rows into a table with a header row.
- Highlight decisions, risks, and recommendations in visually distinct callout boxes.
- Close with a recap section that restates the top three findings and the recommended next step.
